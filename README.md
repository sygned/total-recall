Total Recall is a Chrome (browser) extension that continuously snapshots your open windows and tabs (normal and incognito) so you can restore your previous browsing session tabs after a crash, restart, or accidental close.

<p align='center'>
	<a href="https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/total-recall/cooilemjhdokfnmjpjpdokaboailbhmf">Chrome Store - Total Recall</a>
</p>

To be able to restore incognito windows/tabs, don't forget to check 'Allow incognito' in the extension settings.

## Privacy
Total Recall stores your snapshot entirely on your own device using Chrome's local extension storage. No tab data, URLs, or browsing history ever leaves your browser — nothing is sent to a server, and there is no telemetry or analytics. See the full [privacy policy](https://sygn.net/total-recall/privacy) for details.
